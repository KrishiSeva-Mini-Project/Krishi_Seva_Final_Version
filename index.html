About Us

Krishi Seva is a pan-India electronic trading portal which networks the existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.
VISION
To promote uniformity in agriculture marketing by streamlining of procedures across the integrated markets, removing information asymmetry between buyers and sellers and promoting real time price discovery based on actual demand and supply.
OBJECTIVE
The main objective of this platform to have direct communication between consumers and producers and decide the price on the basis of quality instead of quantity, price will not reduce for farmers due to more growth of the same crop. This will help farmers and consumers
